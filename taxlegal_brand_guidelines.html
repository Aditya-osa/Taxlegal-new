01 · Primary (Light BG)
✓ Primary Use
Use on white (#FFFFFF) or off-white (#F5F7FA) backgrounds. Minimum size: 36px height digital, 15mm print.
02 · Dark Background
✓ On Dark
Use on footer (#1C2535) or dark overlays. Logo text switches to white; red accent X is retained.
03 · Don'ts
✗ Avoid
Do not place on red or mid-blue backgrounds (low contrast). Do not stretch, rotate, or recolour. Do not apply drop shadows. Maintain clear space = logo height × 0.25.

05 · Imagery & Photography
✓ Professional Context

Images showing professionals working — calculators, documents, handshakes, laptops. Conveys trust, expertise, and reliability in the corporate/legal domain.

✓ Colour Treatment

Images may use a cool blue-grey tone consistent with the navy palette. A dark overlay (#1A3A6E at 50–60%) keeps white hero text readable on photos.

✗ Avoid — Casual / Lifestyle Photos

Generic lifestyle imagery unrelated to finance, law, or compliance. Replace with client-facing professional photography that reinforces credibility.

✗ Avoid — Busy Backgrounds under Text

Hero photos must use a dark overlay (navy or charcoal) before placing white text. Minimum contrast ratio 4.5:1 for body copy, 3:1 for large headings.
